Sell your car to us in just 2 simple steps

Selling your car to us is a matter of following 2 simple steps. Just do this and you’re on your way to selling your car to a trusted buyer.

1. Sign Up on our this page and get special Offer page

2. Fill your name, phone number and email Id into the Contact Form and submit it to us


What Next?

What happens after you’ve submitted your contact form? This is what happens:

1. One of our representatives will get in touch with you immediately. Be sure to give clear and accurate information on car brand, model, year, current mileage, previous owners if any and so on

2. We’ll give you a primary quote for your car within 30 minutes.

3. If you’re happy with our quote, book an appointment with us for a free car valuation and we’ll pay a visit to your location.

4. We’ll assess your car thoroughly and determine the amount we can pay for it.

5. If you’re happy with the final price we quote, we can hand over your cheque right there and then. The entire valuation and payment process will not take more than 30 minutes.
